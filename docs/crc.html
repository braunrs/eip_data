The CRCs continue with the EIP-specific EPIC pre-screen, looking for various criteria that would indicate that the CRC could approach the patient to gather more information regarding risk and need for EIP action. The graph and table below shows the number of screens that met the various criteria, and the percentage of the total eligible based on age.

The risk groups that the CRCs are looking for during their Epic pre-screen include: African-American males between the ages of 15-29, Women of Color (WOC), and individuals who identify within the transgender or non-binary community.

The percentages shown below are the percent of the total eligible for EIP screening based on age.
