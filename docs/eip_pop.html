EIP Population

The purpose of this page is to show the demographic population data for those individuals that EIP has worked with for various clinical purposes. This page shows the entire EIP popluation data, and the population data for individuals seen at specific sites is shown on those pages listed under the Sites tab.

Contents

  • Age Distribution
  • Sex and Gender Identity
  • Race and Ethnicity
  • Insurance Coverage
  • Populations of Interest

The information on this page only includes data since the start of 2023. EIP has encountered:

1981 unique individuals.

The demographic information for this EIP population is shown on this page. For further information regarding the population demographics of individuals at particular sites, please refer to those pages listed under the Site tab in the navigation bar.

Age Distribution

This first histogram shows the ages of individuals seen by EIP grouped by age categories. The age categories in this histogram were chosen because they are the categories used in both the CTR and EHE reports to ODH. The total numbers of individuals within each category as well as the percent of the total individuals encountered by EIP are shown in the table.

The following histogram shows the complete age distribution of all individuals that EIP has encountered, with the density curve overlying the histogram.

The following two histograms also show the overall age distribution of the EIP population. They also allow the ability to filter and select based on self-reported gender (first histogram) and the self-reported race (second histogram). You can filter the information by selecting/deselecting the gender/race from the legend on the right side of the graph. If multiple categories are selected, the histogram shows the cumulative total for each age among the categories selected. If you just want to see the particular age distribution for a single gender/race, you can deselect all other options.
